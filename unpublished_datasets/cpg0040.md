## Description

This dataset contains images from an in vitro silver nanoparticle (AgNP) exposure study in Huh-7 human hepatocytes. The goal is to quantify phenotypic and toxicity-related responses using multiplexed image-based assays across two exposure regimens:

Acute exposure (1 day, higher concentration of AgNP) - all batches starting `2022_05`
Repeated exposure (15 days, lower concentration of AgNP) - all batches starting `2022_06`

Cells were stained using three imaging panels:

Live Cell Painting (see [Moraes-Lacerda et al., 2025](https://doi.org/10.21769/BioProtoc.5464))

- Green channel (e.g., 469/525 nm): Acridine Orange (AO) signal predominantly from DNA/RNA (nucleic acids)
- Red channel (e.g., 531/647 nm): Acridine Orange (AO) signal from acidic vesicles (late endosomes and lysosomes, lysosomal compartment)

Cell Painting (modified from [Cimini et al., 2023](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41596-023-00840-9))

- DAPI/Hoechst: nuclei
- Green channel: endoplasmic reticulum
- Red channel: actin / Golgi / plasma membrane
- Cy5: mitochondria

Tox Painting (see [Garcia-Fossa, 2024](https://doi.org/10.47749/T/UNICAMP.2024.1498854))

- DAPI/Hoechst: nuclei
- Green channel: neutral lipids (lipidosis proxy)
- Texas Red: γH2AX (DNA damage proxy)
- Cy5: NFκB (inflammation proxy)
